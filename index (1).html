About Us

The Smart Energy Innovation Hub – Kenya is a blended virtual + physical center connecting communities with clean energy solutions. We partner with county governments, utilities, universities, and private innovators to enable reliable power and productive livelihoods.

Model: Mixed (Portal + Physical Center) — combines a national self‑service portal with county‑level centers for demonstrations, training, and last‑mile support.

Partners

  • County Governments & Rural Electrification Bodies
  • Mini‑grid Developers & Solar Vendors
  • MFIs, SACCOs & Impact Investors
  • Universities & TVETs
  • NGOs & Community‑Based Organizations
